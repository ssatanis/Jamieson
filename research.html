Featured Projects

Automatic Zero-Shot Grading

“Rubrics to Prompts: Assessing Medical Student Post-Encounter Notes with AI„

We developed and deployed a pioneering automatic AI grading system for medical student post-encounter OSCE notes. The system provides not just grades, but also expanding to tailored Narrative Feedback for medical students and educator mentors.

Microsoft Azure Grant, 2023

“Lab awarded Azure credit grant from Microsoft's Accelerating Foundation Models Research initiative to run experiments with GPT-4! Project Title: Rubrics to Prompts: Expert-level Performance Assessment and Feedback in Medical Education with Foundation Models„

Key Achievements

2023

Deployed first AI grading system for OSCE notes

15,000

Medical student examples in our training dataset

Expert-level

Performance achieved in automated assessment

Multimodal

Foundation models for comprehensive analysis

Research Applications

Medical Education

Machine learning algorithms that annotate and encode clinician-patient interactions for medical student education, practice, and testing.

Clinical Oncology

Large language model pipelines for analyzing kidney cancer records and developing bioinformatics workflows for clinical research.

Computer Vision

Interpretable deep learning for cellular properties in label-free live cell images, surgical skill assessment, and 3D medical image analysis.

Key Collaborators

SimCenter - UTSW

Our primary collaboration with the UT Southwestern Simulation Center provides access to vast catalogs of live-action human performance data including video, audio, and text-based inputs from medical student training scenarios.


This partnership enables us to develop and test our AI systems in real-world medical education environments, ensuring practical applicability and immediate impact on student learning outcomes.
